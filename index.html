Expert Advice

Summer Care Tips

Stay fresh, protected and glowing all season long

🧴

Skincare Routine

Apply SPF 50+ sunscreen every 2 hours when outdoors. Don't forget your neck, ears and hands — often missed areas that age quickly.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 3 litres of water daily in summer. Add slices of lemon or cucumber for electrolytes and a refreshing twist.

🕶️

Eye Protection

Wear UV-blocking sunglasses whenever you step outside. UV exposure can cause cataracts and macular degeneration over time.

👗

Dress Smart

Choose loose, light-coloured cotton clothing. Dark fabrics absorb heat, while breathable weaves keep your body temperature regulated.

🌿

Natural Cooling

Use aloe vera gel after sun exposure to soothe skin. Keep a spray bottle with rose water in the fridge for instant cooling mists.

🏃

Exercise Wisely

Work out in the early morning or evening. Avoid midday exercise when UV index peaks between 10AM–4PM for your safety.
